# Template Gallery

Open [`docs/gallery.html`](gallery.html) for the visual gallery.

The gallery explains the three-layer video direction system:

- Story templates define narrative order.
- Scene components define the visual language of each screen.
- Visual themes define the commercial skin.

## Story Templates

### Daily Briefing

Three to five signals with a fast editorial open and a watch-next close.

- Key: `daily_briefing`
- Flow: `cover_hook -> signal_grid -> source_proof -> insight_stack -> next_watch`
- Sample use: Example: three morning AI or market signals, one proof point, one watch-next close.

### Single Event Deep Dive

One story explained through context, mechanism, evidence, and consequence.

- Key: `single_event_deep_dive`
- Flow: `cover_hook -> context_panel -> mechanism_xray -> evidence_quote -> conclusion_stamp`
- Sample use: Example: one launch, policy change, earnings note, or research result explained end to end.

### Breaking Update

Short urgent update with immediate fact, source proof, and next check.

- Key: `breaking_update`
- Flow: `impact_headline -> source_proof -> proof_chips -> next_watch`
- Sample use: Example: a fresh source-backed update that needs a short fact, proof, and next check.

### Product Launch

What shipped, who it helps, proof, and why the workflow changes.

- Key: `product_launch`
- Flow: `product_plate -> before_after_surface -> use_case_grid -> verification_rail -> cta_end`
- Sample use: Example: a new product or feature, who it helps, and what workflow changed.

### Company Update

Entity-led update with action, evidence, impact, and watch signal.

- Key: `company_update`
- Flow: `entity_map -> source_proof -> metric_stack -> risk_matrix -> next_watch`
- Sample use: Example: a company action, source evidence, impact metrics, risks, and next signal.

### Market Radar

Multiple indicators arranged as a calm market board.

- Key: `market_radar`
- Flow: `market_ledger -> pulse_monitor -> signal_grid -> risk_matrix -> conclusion_stamp`
- Sample use: Example: a public market breadth recap with sector strength, risks, and a clean verdict.

### Evidence Chain

Source to claim to validation with proof stops.

- Key: `evidence_chain`
- Flow: `verification_rail -> source_proof -> evidence_quote -> mechanism_xray -> conclusion_stamp`
- Sample use: Example: source to claim to validation, with proof stops and a final confidence read.

### Comparison

Two approaches, products, companies, or signals compared side by side.

- Key: `comparison`
- Flow: `compare_split -> metric_stack -> before_after_surface -> conclusion_stamp`
- Sample use: Example: two tools, companies, assets, or signals compared side by side.

### Timeline

Sequence of milestones and what changes at the end.

- Key: `timeline`
- Flow: `timeline_ribbon -> milestone_stack -> source_proof -> next_watch`
- Sample use: Example: a release sequence, policy timeline, or research-to-product milestone chain.

### Risk Watch

Opportunity, uncertainty, contradiction, and next validation.

- Key: `risk_watch`
- Flow: `risk_matrix -> verification_rail -> proof_chips -> next_watch`
- Sample use: Example: opportunity, contradiction, uncertainty, and the next validation checkpoint.

### Top Five

Ranked countdown for daily or weekly lists.

- Key: `top_five`
- Flow: `ranking_board -> source_proof -> metric_stack -> cta_end`
- Sample use: Example: a ranked daily or weekly list with proof and a compact close.

### Weekly Review

Week-level synthesis across themes, changes, and next week signals.

- Key: `weekly_review`
- Flow: `week_calendar -> signal_grid -> market_ledger -> conclusion_stamp -> next_watch`
- Sample use: Example: a week-level synthesis across themes, changes, and next-week signals.

## Scene Components

### Opening and impact

- `cover_hook` - Cover Hook: Open with one strong claim. Visual grammar: `cinematic_anchor`.
- `impact_headline` - Impact Headline: Make the immediate change obvious. Visual grammar: `cinematic_anchor`.

### Context and insight

- `context_panel` - Context Panel: Set up the story frame. Visual grammar: `runtime_lens`.
- `insight_stack` - Insight Stack: Stack compact insight cards. Visual grammar: `signal_board`.

### Signal groups

- `signal_grid` - Signal Grid: Group several weak signals. Visual grammar: `signal_board`.
- `use_case_grid` - Use Case Grid: Show practical usage lanes. Visual grammar: `official_product_plate`.
- `three_takeaways` - Three Takeaways: Summarize into three useful points. Visual grammar: `signal_board`.

### Numbers and charts

- `metric_stack` - Metric Stack: Stack numbers and short labels. Visual grammar: `market_ledger`.
- `big_number` - Big Number: Feature one number or count. Visual grammar: `data_on_plate`.
- `mini_chart` - Mini Chart: Show a compact trend-like shape. Visual grammar: `data_on_plate`.
- `pulse_monitor` - Pulse Monitor: Show a heartbeat-like signal state. Visual grammar: `data_on_plate`.

### Ledgers and tables

- `market_ledger` - Market Ledger: Present rows of indicators. Visual grammar: `market_ledger`.
- `data_table` - Data Table: Use dense but readable rows. Visual grammar: `market_ledger`.

### Source proof

- `source_proof` - Source Proof: Show the originating source and evidence line. Visual grammar: `verification_rail`.
- `evidence_quote` - Evidence Quote: Pull one source-backed proof sentence. Visual grammar: `verification_rail`.

### Proof chips

- `proof_chips` - Proof Chips: Compress source, date, and tag proof. Visual grammar: `verification_rail`.

### Validation rails

- `verification_rail` - Verification Rail: Move through evidence stops. Visual grammar: `verification_rail`.

### Timelines

- `timeline_ribbon` - Timeline Ribbon: Show milestones along a rail. Visual grammar: `timeline_ribbon`.
- `milestone_stack` - Milestone Stack: Stack phase cards vertically. Visual grammar: `timeline_ribbon`.
- `week_calendar` - Week Calendar: Summarize week-level signals. Visual grammar: `timeline_ribbon`.

### Mechanisms

- `mechanism_xray` - Mechanism X-Ray: Reveal cause and effect layers. Visual grammar: `mechanism_xray`.
- `stack_layers` - Stack Layers: Layer inputs, mechanism, and outcome. Visual grammar: `mechanism_xray`.
- `funnel` - Funnel: Narrow inputs into one outcome. Visual grammar: `mechanism_xray`.

### Comparisons

- `compare_split` - Compare Split: Compare two sides. Visual grammar: `before_after_surface`.
- `before_after_surface` - Before After: Show old state versus new state. Visual grammar: `before_after_surface`.

### Risk matrices

- `risk_matrix` - Risk Matrix: Separate upside, downside, unknowns, and checks. Visual grammar: `verification_rail`.

### Rankings

- `ranking_board` - Ranking Board: Rank selected items. Visual grammar: `market_ledger`.

### Watch lists

- `watchlist` - Watchlist: List what to watch next. Visual grammar: `signal_board`.
- `next_watch` - Next Watch: End with concrete next checks. Visual grammar: `signal_board`.

### Entity maps

- `entity_map` - Entity Map: Show who is connected to the story. Visual grammar: `runtime_lens`.
- `geographic_map` - Geographic Map: Place the story geographically or regionally. Visual grammar: `runtime_lens`.

### Product plates

- `product_plate` - Product Plate: Give a launch or object a premium slot. Visual grammar: `official_product_plate`.

### Conclusions

- `conclusion_stamp` - Conclusion Stamp: Land the verdict cleanly. Visual grammar: `market_ledger`.
- `cta_end` - CTA End: Close with one action or review prompt. Visual grammar: `cinematic_anchor`.

## Visual Themes

- `editorial_dark` - Editorial Dark: Use for serious news, research notes, and night-editorial recaps.
- `executive_light` - Executive Light: Use for boardroom explainers, weekly reviews, and clean business summaries.
- `market_terminal` - Market Terminal: Use for finance, KPI recaps, market breadth, and dense numeric hierarchy.
- `product_keynote` - Product Keynote: Use for product launches, workflow demos, and verified object-led stories.
- `data_magazine` - Data Magazine: Use for analytical features, chart-led explainers, and slower editorial pacing.
- `social_pop` - Social Pop: Use for countdowns, lighter recaps, and high-energy social packages.
