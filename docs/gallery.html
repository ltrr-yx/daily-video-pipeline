Optional Illustration / 可选插图

如果有生图模型，插图可以怎样增强镜头

如果用户有 GPT Image 或其他图片生成模型，插图层只负责氛围、对象、空间和隐喻。标题、数字、图表、来源、引用和结论仍由脚本与渲染器生成，这样画面更好看，同时不会让图片模型编造事实。

Motion Grammar / 出场语法

再约束元素如何进入和组装

Motion 层不是装饰说明，而是代码里的渲染合同。它规定先出现结构、轨道、数据还是结论，让元素出场符合叙事逻辑。

soft_assemblycover, context, grid

Soft Assembly

A calm commercial default: shell first, title second, cards or proof details stagger into a readable hold.

Entrance soft fade with a small upward settle
Emphasis staggered content assembly
Exit short fade-through
evidence_traceproof, chips, rail, timeline

Evidence Trace

Proof-oriented motion: draw the rail or path first, then reveal stops, labels, and evidence cards in sequence.

Entrance rail draw-on before nodes
Emphasis node and card stagger
Exit source line holds before fade-through
product_revealproduct, map

Product Reveal

A product or object gets a premium reveal with a soft plate entrance, light camera push, and restrained callouts.

Entrance matte-like plate reveal with gentle scale
Emphasis callout pins after the anchor is visible
Exit slow push into the final product read
data_tweenmetric, ledger, ranking

Data Tween

Numbers, rows, and tiny charts animate as evidence changes rather than appearing as static slides.

Entrance metric rows stagger in
Emphasis value and sparkline fill
Exit final values hold long enough to read
mechanism_scanmechanism, split, matrix

Mechanism Scan

Layered explanations reveal structure before claims, then use a scan or connector pass to show causality.

Entrance layer peel or split reveal
Emphasis focus rail, divider, or scan pass
Exit consequence layer settles last
verdict_locklist, stamp

Verdict Lock

Conclusion motion compresses supporting details into a final practical watch item or verdict.

Entrance verdict plate enters after the setup
Emphasis supporting checks stagger below
Exit final stamp hold
