Composition Rule

One video direction is a simple product of three decisions.

Pick one story template, fill it with scene components, then apply one visual theme. Example: market_radar + market_ledger / signal_grid / verification_rail + market_terminal creates a public market recap without exposing private holdings or private source lists.
